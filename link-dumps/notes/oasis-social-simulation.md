# OASIS: Open Agents Social Interaction Simulations

**URL:** https://oasis.camel-ai.org
**Type:** Research Tool / Simulation Platform

## Full Name

OASIS (Open Agents Social Interaction Simulations on One Million Agents)

## Description

OASIS is "a generalizable and scalable social media simulator" designed to model large-scale user interactions on platforms like X and Reddit.

## Capabilities

- **Scale:** Can simulate up to one million agents simultaneously
- **Features:**
  - Dynamic social networks
  - Diverse user actions
  - Recommendation algorithms

## Research Applications

Study complex social phenomena:
- Information spreading
- Group polarization
- Herd behavior
- Multi-scale social dynamics

## Relevance

Major tool for studying emergent social behaviors in AI-driven social networks. Important for understanding information dynamics and social influence at scale.
