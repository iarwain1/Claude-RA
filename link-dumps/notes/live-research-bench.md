# LiveResearchBench

**URL:** https://livedeepresearch.github.io/
**Type:** Benchmark

## Description

This benchmark evaluates deep research systems that produce comprehensive, citation-grounded reports by searching and synthesizing information from live web sources.

## Key Features

- **Task Set:** 100 expert-curated tasks
- **Domains:** Daily life, enterprise, and academia
- **Evaluation:** Assesses extensive research requiring real-time information retrieval and analysis
- **Framework:** DeepEval evaluation system

## Evaluation Metrics

DeepEval measures:
- Content quality
- Citation accuracy
- Report organization

**Systems Evaluated:** 17 frontier deep research systems

## Relevance

Important benchmark for evaluating AI research assistants. Tests ability to conduct comprehensive research with proper citations and synthesis from live web data.
